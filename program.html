My Program

I am going to write a program where the user can play a game of blackjack alongside some other players controlled by the computer, and the program will use card counting and probabilities to recommend a move. I will write it in Python adn will use modules such as tkinter to create an interactive GUI. The user majority of the user's window will hold the game, and at the side the recommendations and counts will be displayed.

OOP

I will use Object Oriented Programming to create a class for a Deck of Cards, then sub-classes of a Card and a Hand. I may add some other sub-classes if thye would benefit the program. I would also create classes for the differnet types of Player: User Player, Computer Player and Dealer. There could be a super-class of Player adn sub-classes for all the types. This would mean I could customise the methods that each of them have to fit the role each is intended to fill. The Dealer and Computer Player would have to have conditions for which they choose their moves in the game, but I can bind the User Player methods to buttins on the GUI./p>

Predicting cards and recommnednig moves

To keep a count of the cards, my program will a combination of card counting techniques alongside tracking what cards have already been dealt to make an estimate of what card will appear next. It will also look for the next most likely cards and the most likely scores of carsd to display to the user. It will also refer ot basic strategy and compare with what cards are likely to be dealt next to then recommend a move to the user.
